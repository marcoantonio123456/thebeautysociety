Relax & Renew

Our experts provide an array of injection services such as Botox, Dysport, Dermal Fillers, PRP , and others that all reduce the appearance of ageing and give your face, hair and body smoother and younger appearance.

Services

Botox

Targeting frown lines, repeated muscle contractions, frowning and squinting. Botox works beneath the skin’s surface and targets the underlying muscle activity.

Dysport

Reducing wrinkles, Dysport effectively reduces the activity of muscles underneath the skin to create a smooth and refreshed appearance.

Dermal Fillers

Our Injectable Fillers are used to give volume and create contours on the face where needed or where the volume has disappeared with age. This non-surgical procedure can result in a younger, refreshed, and revitalized appearance.

PRP

Platelet Rich Plasma Therapy, an effective hair loss treatment. Stimulating healing where it is otherwise failing.

Lip Injections

Lip Augmentation, giving you fuller, plumper lips. Adding shape, structure and volume.

Microneedling

A minimally invasive procedure, generating new collagen giving your skin tissue a smoother, firmer, more toned skin.

Enhancement Expertise

Our experienced team set the highest standards in medical enhancements and aim to exceed our patient’s expectations. We will conduct a thorough consultation and explain your options and suggestions to make sure that you are comfortable with your enhancement plan.
